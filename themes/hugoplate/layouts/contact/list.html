How to Apply

  • CV or résumé outlining your academic background, research experience, and technical skills.
  • Statement of purpose describing your research interests and motivation for joining HDIL.
  • Portfolio or GitHub link (optional) demonstrating relevant projects or code.

Who Should Apply

  • Students interested in human-centered data, visualization, or AI-driven systems.
  • Applicants with experience or strong interest in HCI, data analysis, or interactive systems.
  • Self-motivated individuals comfortable tackling challenging research problems.
  • Proficiency in Python, Kotlin, JavaScript, or similar programming languages.
  • Motivation to publish in leading venues (e.g., CHI, UIST, VIS, TVCG).

Questions?
